Research

I am a graduate student in the DCS Theory Group at the University of Toronto, where I am very grateful to be supervised by Stephen Cook and Toni Pitassi. My current research interests lie in different aspects of computational complexity and algorithms, with a focus in circuit complexity. I also have an interest in the intersection of computer science and cognitive science (my primary collaboraters here are Tarek Besold and Todd Wareham). My last project in this area saw the application of concepts in computational complexity to models of metaphor and concept re-representation.
